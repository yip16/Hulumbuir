Hulumbuir

Hulunbuir is located in the northeastern part of Inner Mongolia, adjacent to Russia and Mongolia. It is a prefecture-level city with the largest land area and the northernmost geographical location in China. The vast praries, inaccessible forests, numerous rivers, lakes and wetlands, rich natural landscapes and unique ethnic customs endow Hulunbuirwith the reputation of "the pearl on the cockscomb" of China's territory.

In the summer of 2021, a group of seven of us went to Hulunbuir for a week.

On the prairie, we experienced various novel projects, such as archery, milking cows, grass sliding, and touching calves and lambs up close.

This trip is definitely worth it! We not only experienced the natural scenery, but also the local customs. And we have also learned how to protect nature and prevent this natural landscape from gradually failing.
